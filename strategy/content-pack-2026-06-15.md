## 5. THE 2-3x LEVER

This content lifts CTR and lowers CPA at the top of the funnel, but the cheapest, most reliable path to the 2-3x ROAS target is the checkout. With 61.8% of carts abandoning at checkout, the funnel is leaking the buyers the ads already paid to acquire; fixing that one step (faster checkout, clearer ZAR pricing, the money-back guarantee surfaced at the payment moment, fewer form fields, restored payment trust signals) recovers conversions at near-zero incremental ad cost and can lift ROAS from roughly 1.5x toward ~2.4x on the same spend. Content gets the click; the checkout fix is what makes 2-3x reliable rather than a lucky-creative spike. **Kill/scale rule:** kill any ad set under 1.25x ROAS after 3 days; scale (raise budget) any set holding above 1.8x.
