## 0. The Only Numbers That Govern Decisions

| Metric | Value | Rule |
|---|---|---|

| True blended MER (real Shopify rev / total spend) | ~2.5x | This is the scoreboard, not pixel ROAS |

| Working engine | ABO Warm Up, 6 sets, 1.48 ROAS 7d / 1.65 today | Scale this |
| Leak | CBO Scale, 0.64 ROAS 7d on R4k | Fix or defund TODAY |

**Judge everything on a 7-day rolling window, by blended MER and net cash.** Daily pixel ROAS is noise. The blended MER floor for the whole account is **2.0x**. Above 2.0x you scale. Between 1.5x and 2.0x you hold. Below 1.5x you cut, you do not add budget.

## 2. Creative Cadence

Creative volume is the constraint, so this is the most important section. Statics are cheaper, faster, and the algorithm favours them. Lead with statics, prove the angle, then convert winners to video.

### Weekly output target
- **2 new test batches per week** (one per upload-day half, but ship both on the single upload day, see §6).
- **5-6 creatives per batch.**
- **Static-to-video ratio: 70/30.** So a 6-creative batch = ~4 statics + 2 videos.
- **Net new test sets per week: 2** (one batch = one ABO test set).
- **Diversity beats clones.** Each batch must contain genuinely different angles, not 6 colour-swaps of one image. If two creatives share the same hook, they are one creative.

### Angle bank (rotate, never repeat the same 5)
Pull from these so every batch is genuinely different:
- "R10,000,000+ generated by SA students" (social proof / scale)
- Janko: R40k first month, R4k on day two (named-result proof)
- "We build it FOR you" (Elite R999, done-for-you)
- "Built live on Discord" (transparency / community)
- Price-anchor: "A real online store from R299" (Starter accessibility)
- "Income beyond your salary" (problem/aspiration, NO income guarantee)
- Real SA supplier / local fulfilment (trust, anti-scam)
- Founder/team POV talking-head (authenticity)

### Horizontal then vertical (iterate winners)
- **Horizontal first:** spray many different angles wide and cheap. Find which angle hits.
- **Then vertical:** take the winning angle and make 4-6 variations of IT, new hook frame, new opening 3s, new format (static→video), new caption, UGC vs designed. You are mining the vein, not digging new holes.
- A winner is not "done." A winner gets a vertical batch within the same week.

### Zombie-campaign move (rescue high-conviction no-spend ads)
When an ad you believe in gets zero/near-zero spend inside a multi-ad set (algorithm starved it before it could prove itself):
1. Pull that single ad into its own fresh ABO ad set ("zombie set").
2. Give it a clean R150/day at the ad-set level so it cannot be starved.
3. Let it run 3 days. If it converts, promote to Scale. If not, kill, now you know it was the creative, not the starvation.

## 3. Budget Scaling, Aggressive but Safe

Start at ~R2,400/day. Ramp on the back of blended MER, not vibes.

### The gate (check before EVERY step up)
- **7-day blended MER ≥ 2.0x** → you may scale.
- **1.5x-2.0x** → hold, do not add budget, push creative instead.
- **< 1.5x** → cut the worst set, do not scale anything.

### Step sizes
- **Test sets:** never increase. They live and die at R150-R250. Scaling happens in the Scale campaign, not Test.
- **Scale campaign (unproven promotions):** +20% every 3 days while MER holds ≥ 2.0x. Small steps protect the learning phase.
- **Proven winners (Image Batch #1, Video Batch #5 type, holding 3x+ for 5+ days):** bolder. +30-40% every 3 days, or duplicate the winning set at 1.5x its budget rather than editing the original (preserves the learning on the original).

### Concrete 14-day ramp (illustrative, MER-gated at each step)
| Day | Total daily spend | Move | Gate to proceed |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | R2,400 | Pause old CBO, launch new Scale CBO R1,500 + Test R900 |, |
| 3 | R2,900 | +20% Scale if 7d MER ≥ 2.0 | MER ≥ 2.0 |
| 6 | R3,500 | +20% Scale, fold in Test winners | MER ≥ 2.0 |
| 9 | R4,300 | +30% on proven winners only | Winners 3x+ 5d |
| 12 | R5,200 | +20% Scale + 1 new Test batch budget | MER ≥ 2.0 |
| 14 | R6,000+ | Hold, reassess 7d net cash | Net cash positive |

If MER dips under 2.0x at any step, **freeze the budget at the prior level** until the 7-day window recovers. You never skip a step up; you also never push through a failing gate.

## 4. Kill / Scale Rules

### Kill an ad when:
- **Spend hits 2x your target cost-per-purchase with zero sales.** Target CPP ≈ breakeven. If CPP target is R250 and an ad spends R500 with no purchase, kill it.
- It has had a fair shot: at least ~R150-R200 spend AND 1-2 days. Do not kill at R40.

### Leading indicators (act before the kill threshold):
- **Cost per Add-to-Cart** climbing well above set average → weak intent, likely dead.
- **Cost per Checkout-initiated** high with ATCs present → price/offer friction, not a hook problem.
- **CTR (link) under ~1%** → the hook is not landing. Low CTR + high CPM = the creative is the problem.
- **CTR healthy but no sales** → landing page / offer / tracking issue, not the ad. Do not kill the ad; check the funnel.

### Do NOT:
- Panic-edit inside the learning phase (first ~3 days / ~50 conversions). Every edit resets learning and wastes the spend so far.
- Judge on a single bad day. **Zoom out to 7 days** before any kill or scale call. Today's 1.65 vs the 7-day 1.48 is exactly why: the day is noise, the week is signal.

### Scale an ad when:
- 7-day blended MER on the set ≥ 2.0x AND it has exited learning AND it has held for 3+ days. Then feed via §3.

## 5. Guardrails (Flagged-Risk Account)

This account is flagged. One ban resets everything. Non-negotiable:

- **Do NOT spin up a new ad account or pixel to "escape" limits.** That reads as ban-evasion and gets the whole Business Manager nuked. Scale inside the existing account.
- **Keep a warm backup Business Manager**, separate, aged, real activity, its own page and payment method. Warm, not used. Insurance only.
- **Refund rate is a kill-switch.** Keep it **under 0.7%.** Above that, Meta and the PSP both start freezing. Watch it weekly. High refunds from one product = pull the product, not just the ad.
- **No income-claim creative. Ever.** "R10m generated by students" and "Janko did R40k" are factual student results, framed as results, not promises. Never "you will make X." Never guaranteed income. This is both a Meta policy line and the honest offer.
- **Fix CAPI / tracking before you scale.** The machine optimises on what it is fed. Pixel under-reports because WhatsApp sales close off-pixel. Get server-side CAPI firing Purchase events (including WhatsApp-closed sales where possible) so the algorithm sees the real conversions. Scaling a half-blind pixel wastes the ramp.
- **Cashflow float gap.** PSP payouts lag (typically T+1 to T+7). If you ramp from R2.4k to R6k/day in two weeks, you front the spend now and collect revenue later. **Hold 10-14 days of spend as float** (at R6k/day that is ~R60k-R84k) before the aggressive steps, or the account starves itself on cash, not performance.

## 6. Weekly Rhythm

**Upload Day, Monday.** Ship both new test batches (10-12 creatives total), launch zombie sets, promote any winners into Scale. One upload day keeps learning phases clean and stops mid-week panic-edits.

**Deep-Dive Day, Friday.** No new launches. Pure analysis on the 7-day window:

| Check | What to look at | Action trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Blended MER (7d) | Shopify rev / total spend | < 2.0 hold, < 1.5 cut |
| Net cash (7d) | Revenue minus spend minus VAT minus refunds | Negative = stop ramp |
| CPP per set | vs ~R250 target | 2x + no sale = kill |
| CTR / CPM per ad | Hook health | < 1% CTR = creative dead |
| Cost per ATC / Checkout | Funnel leading indicators | Spike = pre-kill warning |
| Refund rate | All orders 7d | > 0.7% = freeze + investigate |
| Winners | Which angles holding 3x+ | Build vertical batch next Mon |
| Pixel/CAPI health | Events firing, match quality | Any gap = fix before scaling |

Mid-week (Wed): a 5-minute glance only. Kill anything past 2x CPP with no sale. Otherwise hands off.
